History of Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith. It has grown to become one of the most popular sports in the world, with professional leagues like the NBA captivating millions of fans.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has five players on the court.
  • The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop.
  • Games are divided into quarters, and the team with the most points at the end wins.

Famous Players

Some legendary basketball players include Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, and Shaquille O'Neal.
